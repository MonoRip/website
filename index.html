What is Among Us VR?

Among Us VR is a VR game based on the popular game "Among Us". It is a game where you play as a crewmate or an imposter on a spaceship. The goal of the crewmates is to complete tasks and find the imposter. The goal of the imposter is to kill all the crewmates without being caught.

What is the release date?

Among Us VR is currently in development and is expected to be released in 2021.
